The film's launch event with a puja ceremony was held on 11 November 2018, which saw the attendance of noted personalities from the Telugu film industry,[58] including Prabhas and Rana Daggubati, who had worked with Rajamouli in the Baahubali franchise.[59][60] Principal photography of the film commenced on 19 November 2018 in Hyderabad at a specially erected set at an Aluminium factory in the location.[50][51] It is the first Indian film to be shot on Arri Alexa LF and Arri Signature Prime lens.[61] Senthil Kumar told American Cinematographer that, he "avoided the use of overt stylization such as extreme color palettes and unrealistic camera moves, in favor of a more classical cinematographic approach".[62] The filming schedule was completed on 6 December 2018.[63] The team took a brief break before commencing the film's second schedule on 21 January 2019 at Ramoji Film City in Hyderabad.
